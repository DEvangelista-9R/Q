Your Name

"Your Name" or "Kimi no Na wa" is a movie about Taki and Mitsuha two high schoolers trying to find eachother because of a memory and an unsolved mystery from the past. Watch them through this back and forth of emotions and confusion as try to find eachother, a simple concept but such a wonderful execution.
